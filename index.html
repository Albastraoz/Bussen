Rules

This card game is all about luck! The goal is to reach the last round in which you have to guess the color of the card every round. If your guess is wrong you will need to do a specific action which will be specified for each round.

In the top of the screen you will be able to change color from 'RED' to 'BLACK' with black as default.

TIP: counting cards is possible!
